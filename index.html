About Our Clinic

We Care About Your Dental Health

Asnan Dental Care is Gerugambakkam's most trusted dental clinic, proudly serving patients from Porur, Ramapuram, Valasaravakkam, and Virugambakkam for over 7 years. Led by Dr. Nawazuddin B.D.S, our clinic combines advanced dental technology with a gentle, patient-first approach.
